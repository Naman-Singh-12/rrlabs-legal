Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how RR Labs ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in the Tap On Gray mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

The App currently operates entirely offline and locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

The following data is stored only on your device and never leaves it:

  • The display name you choose (stored locally)
  • Your game scores, accuracy, and reaction time statistics
  • Your selected game mode and app preferences (theme, sound, vibration)
  • Game session history
  • Any feedback you write inside the App (stored locally only)

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Camera, microphone, or contacts

3. Third-Party Services

The current version of the App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks. No data is shared with any third party.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the App is safe for users of all ages.

5. Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device using the device's own storage system. We have no access to this data. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when new features are added (such as cloud sync or analytics). The updated date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest version. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
